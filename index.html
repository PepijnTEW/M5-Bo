Who are We?

Welcome to Unity a Amsterdam Student Association for Mbo Students! We are a dynamic community of students from diverse backgrounds, united by our passion for learning and engagement. Our association provides a platform for academic collaboration, social activities, and personal growth. From workshops and networking events to cultural outings and volunteer opportunities, we strive to enrich the student experience in Amsterdam. Join us to connect with like-minded peers, develop your skills, and make the most of your time in this vibrant city!

Events

We’re thrilled to announce a lineup of upcoming events designed to engage, inspire, and connect our members. Join us for our monthly networking night, where you can meet industry professionals and fellow students over refreshments. Don’t miss our interactive workshops on topics like resume building and interview skills, perfect for preparing you for the job market. Plus, we’ll be hosting a cultural outing to explore the city’s rich history and vibrant arts scene. Stay tuned for dates and details—let’s make the most of our time together in Amsterdam!
